A neighbor's idea

The Blessing Box

One of LCR's most-loved outreach spots started with a teenager. In 2020, seventeen-year-old Isabella Pawloski learned that one in four children in Pinellas County faces food insecurity, and she decided that enough was enough.

Using old newspaper boxes donated by the Tampa Bay Times, she repainted them into what she calls Blessing Boxes. The idea is simple: if you can, drop off non-perishable food; if you need a little help, take what you need, no questions asked. The first Blessing Box stands at LCR in Palm Harbor. This story is adapted from a BayNews9 community report in 2020.

In the community

Serve with others

Through service and outreach, LCR shares God's love and discovers God in our midst. The Global Missions team supports neighbors near and far, from the F.E.A.S.T. food pantry in Palm Harbor to backpacks for Pinellas County students, Angel Tree gifts at Christmas, and the Souper Bowl of Caring.

A seat at the table

A full meal, dine in or take out

Wednesday Night Out is open to the public in the LCR Social Hall. You can settle in at a table with friends or drive through for take-out on your way home.

Each meal includes salad, an entree, sides, a beverage, and dessert for $8, cooked from time-tested recipes. Pick-up runs from 3:30 to 6:30, so there is room in the evening for everyone.

How to order

Pre-order by noon on Wednesday

To help the kitchen plan, pre-order by noon on Wednesday. Text Chef Joe at 727-678-2898 with your name, the number of meals you would like, and your estimated pick-up time.

The weekly menu is posted on LCR's Facebook page, so you can see what is cooking before you order.

Why it matters

A meal that feeds the neighborhood

Every Wednesday Night Out meal does more than fill a plate. Proceeds help feed 100 hungry people through the Shepherd Center in Tarpon Springs, and through networking, more than 150 additional households across Pinellas County.

Come for the food and the fellowship, and know that your meal helps a neighbor eat too.

Wednesdays and Mondays

Bible Study

LCR's Bible Study is a lively, curious conversation about Scripture and the big questions of faith. Everyone is welcome, whether you have studied the Bible for years or are opening it for the first time.

The group meets in person and on Zoom on Wednesdays from 10 a.m. to 12 p.m. and Mondays at 6:30 p.m., except on council-meeting Mondays. Recordings are shared on LCR's YouTube channel.

How we study

Documentary-style, with the big questions in view

The Wednesday morning group is taught a bit like a National Geographic, Discovery Channel, or Smithsonian program, with an eye on the major theological questions each passage raises as the text unfolds.

Right now the group is working through the book Matthew by Thomas Long. Contact the church office for a copy of the book, which is $5.

You do not need to bring anything but curiosity. Come with your questions and join the conversation.

Join from anywhere

In person and on Zoom

Bible Study meets in person and continues to be shared on Zoom, so you can take part from home when you need to.

To join on Zoom, use Meeting ID 814 2090 9195 and Passcode 230784. If you need help logging in, contact the church office and someone will walk you through it.

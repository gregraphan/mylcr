Caretaking of Creation Emphasis

From Crowd Events like Paws for Praise (Oct, 2015) to team meetings to worship you may see our behaviors/practices fine-tuned to pay attention to this emphasis. You will likely see recycling used more, conservation practices considered, worship service bulletins shortened or if possible eliminated and so on. Paws for Praise was a direct example of this emphasis; most of LCR’s efforts in 2015’s God’s Work, Our Hands Sunday highlighted stewardship of creation.

During the recent Dreamleaders Initiative weekend, eager support for a neighborhood recycling center, vegetable garden and pollinator garden was in evidence.

Watch for how this emphasis shows itself as LCR “greens” up for the future!

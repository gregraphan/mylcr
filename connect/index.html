Friendship and faith

Start where you feel at home

It can be challenging to open ourselves up to new relationships, and making new friendships often takes intentional effort. The good news is that God is always at work, with something next in store, so we can relax a little as life changes and newness comes.

Take time to relish and cherish the gifts that are our friends and companions along the journey.

Ways to gather

Groups and gatherings

LCR's life together includes many small groups that meet through the week. Bible Study gathers Wednesdays from 10 a.m. to noon and Mondays at 6:30 p.m., Centering Prayer meets Mondays at 7 p.m., and Wednesday Night Out serves a shared meal every Wednesday.

There is more to join: Mahjongg on Fridays at 9 a.m., Bunco on the second Tuesday of the month at 7 p.m., and the Resurrection Readers book club on the first Tuesday at 1 p.m. in Room 9. You do not need to be a member to come and see. The ministries page lists each group with its day and contact.

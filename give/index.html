Give

Thank you for supporting Lutheran Church of the Resurrection.

Your gifts help spread Christ's love in our community and around the world.

Continue to Realm

Ways to give

Give in worship or online

There are a few simple ways to give. During worship, you can place a gift in the offering as the plate is passed. Thank you for giving as you are able.

Offering envelopes help the church office keep track of a member's giving. Ask the office if you would like some. You can also give online through Realm, the church's giving platform, at any time.

Online giving

Continue through Realm

Give securely through Realm, the church's giving platform. You can make a one-time gift or set up regular giving that fits your life.

Every gift helps LCR share Christ's love in the community and around the world, from neighbors nearby to partners farther away. If you have a question about giving, the church office is glad to help.

Members

Member account access

If you are an LCR member, Realm is also where you manage your church account. You can check upcoming events, update your giving, and see your giving statement whenever you need it.

Member resources connect you to Realm for these tasks.

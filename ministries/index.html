Find Your Place

The Holy Spirit works in each person in one way or another for the good of all.

LCR ministry life includes worship and music, prayer and study, service, fellowship, hospitality, campus care, stewardship, and community outreach.

Ask About a Ministry

Many ways to take part

Bring your gifts

There are different kinds of gifts, but the same Holy Spirit gives them. Across LCR you will find opportunities in worship and music, faith and prayer, service and outreach, fellowship, and congregational support.

Music runs through worship at LCR, from choir and band to instrumental groups that help the congregation sing.

Your next step

Start with a conversation

Every ministry welcomes new people, and you can begin simply by reaching out. Tell the church office what you enjoy or care about, and someone will help you find a good fit.

Put Faith into Action

Ministries at LCR

Explore ministry areas and contact the church when you find a place you would like to join.

Worship and music

  • Contemporary Worship

    Creates the slides and bulletins that support contemporary worship and helps prepare the space for the congregation.

  • Traditional Worship Team

    Plans and prepares the traditional service so the congregation can gather with ease.

  • Music

    Welcomes vocal and instrumental musicians to help lead the congregation in song.

  • Worship Role Volunteers

    Reads, ushers, and fills the many small roles that help a service run smoothly.

Faith and prayer

  • Bible Study

    Explores Scripture like a documentary, digging into the big theological questions each text raises.

  • Centering Prayer

    Gathers for silent meditative prayer, contemplative walks, and wisdom sharing that support the Christian spiritual journey.

  • Stewardship

    Explores how our gifts, vocation, finances, and care for creation shape faithful living.

Service and outreach

  • Bread Ministry

    Bakes fresh loaves offered as welcome gifts to visitors each weekend, building relationships one loaf at a time.

  • Global Missions

    Provides help to people in need, supporting the F.E.A.S.T. food pantry, school backpacks, Angel Tree gifts, and the Souper Bowl of Caring.

  • Metropolitan Volunteer Partners

    Serves alongside Metropolitan Ministries at the Miracles of Pasco facility, offering hope to single parents and families.

  • Outreach Group

    Builds neighborhood connections through outreach and shared resources.

  • Stitches

    Sews and crochets quilts, caps, and other items of comfort for the church and community.

  • Community Together Ministry Team

    A social ministry that partners with people who are homeless or under-served to provide food, clothing, and support.

Fellowship and generations

  • Book Club

    Brings people together to expand literary horizons, make new friends, and enjoy good conversation.

    Explore Resurrection Readers →

  • Bunco

    Offers women's fellowship through a monthly dice game, with time for Bible study and support of church needs.

  • Mahjongg

    Gathers to play, laugh, and learn a new game together. Newcomers are welcome.

  • Wednesday Night Out

    Shares a weekly meal open to the public, with proceeds that help feed hungry neighbors through the Shepherd Center and area networks.

  • Women's Night Out

    Connects women through fellowship and service.

  • Youth and Families

    Creates opportunities for young people and families to grow in faith.

Congregational support

  • Faith Community Volunteers

    Supports spiritual and physical health, helping with home and hospital visits, staying in touch by phone, and encouraging healthy living. You do not need to be a nurse.

  • Finance

    Oversees the congregation's financial health, reporting, and careful stewardship of resources.

  • Hospitality

    Hosts gatherings through the year, from the Spring Luncheon to the Fall Potluck, and supports the Christmas concert and dinner.

  • Mission Endowment Committee

    Supports grants that extend LCR's mission.

  • Property Group

    Cares for LCR's buildings, campus, and equipment.

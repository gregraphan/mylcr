Find Your Place

The Holy Spirit works in each person in one way or another for the good of all.

LCR ministry life includes worship and music, prayer and study, service, fellowship, hospitality, campus care, stewardship, and community outreach.

Ask About a Ministry

Many ways to take part

Bring your gifts

There are different kinds of gifts, but the same Holy Spirit gives them. Across LCR you will find opportunities in worship and music, faith and prayer, service and outreach, fellowship, and congregational support.

Music runs through worship at LCR, from choir and band to instrumental groups that help the congregation sing.

Service

Serve with your hands

From church work days to God's Work Our Hands projects, LCR volunteers build, repair, and lend a hand together. Bring whatever skills you have, or come to learn alongside others.

See Ways to Serve

Outreach

Reach our neighbors

LCR shares God's love beyond its walls through mission and outreach. Volunteers fill backpacks with school supplies for Pinellas County students, keep the Blessing Box stocked, and support neighbors near and far.

Meet Community Partners

Fellowship and crafts

Make something together

Crafters gather to create handmade cards, quilts, and seasonal gifts, sharing good conversation along the way. The Crafty Ladies also fill the church with a handmade Winter Show.

See Church Life

Worship and music

Lift every voice

A praise band and vocalists help lead contemporary songs, while the choir and instrumental groups bring their gifts to the seasons of worship. There is a place for many kinds of musicians.

Every ministry welcomes new people, and you can begin simply by reaching out. Tell the church office what you enjoy or care about, and someone will help you find a good fit.

Through the year

Seasonal worship and annual events

Some of LCR's most-loved gatherings come around each year, with exact dates announced each season on the church's Facebook page.

On Christmas Eve, LCR offers a 4 p.m. family service with a children's sermon, communion, carols, and candlelight, and a 7 p.m. service of carols, communion, and candlelight. On Christmas Day, come as you are, pajamas welcome, for worship at 10 a.m. with plenty of caroling.

Each November, the Crafty Ladies Annual Winter Show fills the church with handmade wreaths, ornaments, jewelry, and more from dozens of vendors. It is held rain or shine, indoors and out, with food available and free admission.
